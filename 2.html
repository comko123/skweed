History Of Nike(나이키의 역사)

In 1972, the brand name was changed to Nike.

after the American pronunciation of Nike, the goddess of victory in Greek mythology.

History Of Nike air cushioning technology

Air cushioning technology originated from the idea of ​​Frank Rudy

an employee of the National Aeronautics and Space Administration, and was first devised
in 1979 using the property of compressed air to return to its original state even under external pressure.


Nike created a shoe sole that naturally reverts to its original state by infusing compressed air into a tight pocket.
